Meet Our Foster Friends

Whiskers

A gentle soul who loves sunny spots and chin scratches. Whiskers is always ready for a quiet cuddle session.

Mittens

Playful and curious, Mittens will steal your heart with her silly antics and love for chasing feather toys.

Biscuit

Named for her love of kneading, Biscuit is a sweet lap cat who purrs the moment you sit down.

Shadow

Mysterious and elegant, Shadow enjoys observing from high perches and will warm up to you with patience.

Pumpkin

A total sweetheart with a big personality. Pumpkin greets everyone with headbutts and loud purrs.

Luna

Calm and affectionate, Luna loves to curl up beside you and watch the world go by from the window.
