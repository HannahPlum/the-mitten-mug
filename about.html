Cats, Coffee, and Where it All Began

Our Love for Cats

The Mitten Mug was born from a simple idea: what if your coffee break could also brighten a cat's day? We partner with local shelters to give foster cats a cozy place to socialize and find their forever homes. Every purr, every playful pounce, and every quiet nap happens right here in our cafe.

Our resident cats are more than just adorable companions—they're family waiting to happen. Since opening our doors, we've helped dozens of cats find loving homes while serving up smiles to our guests.

Rooted in Coffee

Great coffee is at the heart of everything we do. We source our beans from local Orlando roasters who share our passion for quality and community. From rich espressos to creamy lattes, every cup is crafted with care.

Whether you're here for a quick pick-me-up or a leisurely afternoon with a furry friend, our baristas are dedicated to making your experience memorable—one sip at a time.

Come See Us

We're located in the heart of Orlando, just minutes from downtown. Whether you're a local or visiting the area, we'd love to welcome you and our furry friends.
